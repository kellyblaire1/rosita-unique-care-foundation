About Us

Rosita Unique Care Foundation (RUCF) was set up as a result of the endemic levels of vulnerability observed among vulnerable groups in the society.

This group includes children, youth, widows and other women in the society. She has this group as her main population of interest. RUCF commenced on 8th August 2007 as a humanitarian, not-for-profit, nongovernmental community based organization, and has her office located along Ibeafor Road, Ezenaobom Umunumo, Ehime Mbano Local Government Area of Imo State in Nigeria.
