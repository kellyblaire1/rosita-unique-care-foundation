About Us

Rosita Unique Care Foundation (RUCF) was set up as a result of the endemic levels of vulnerability observed among vulnerable groups in the society.

This group includes children, youth, widows and other women in the society. She has this group as her main population of interest. RUCF commenced on 8th August 2007 as a humanitarian, not-for-profit, nongovernmental community based organization, and has her office located along Ibeafor Road, Ezenaobom Umunumo, Ehime Mbano Local Government Area of Imo State in Nigeria.
We are registered with the Imo State Ministry of Women Affairs and Social Development with certificate number 0055, and also registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission with registration number, 052339.
Rosita Unique Care Foundation has a five-man Board of Trustees that formulates policies, but the day-to-day operation of the organization is managed by the officers of the organization led by the Improvement Manager under the supervision of the Executive Director. The current staff strength is eight, made up of regular and part-time staff with thirty-three dedicated volunteers.
